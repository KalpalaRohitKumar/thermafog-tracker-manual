1. Introduction

The ThermaFog Tracker is an IoT-based monitoring solution designed for thermal fogging machines. It enables real-time device status monitoring, fuel status detection, location tracking, and IoT-based reporting.

The system integrates on-board sensors, 4G connectivity, and a monitoring dashboard to provide reliable field-level visibility and actionable operational insights.

2. Safety Guidelines

  • Handle the device carefully. Do not drop or throw it.
  • Do not attempt to disassemble or reassemble the device.
  • Do not expose the device to water or moisture.
  • Keep the device out of reach of children.
  • Do not dispose of the device in fire.

3. Product Overview

  • Device power status monitoring
  • Petrol and diesel solenoid status
  • GNSS-based location tracking
  • Operational event logging
  • Cloud-based reporting via cellular network

4. Operating Instructions

Before Using the Device
  • First power ON the device, not the petrol or diesel solenoids.
  • If the petrol solenoid is OFF, the diesel solenoid cannot be switched ON.
  1. After powering ON the device, the petrol solenoid turns ON first.
  2. The diesel solenoid turns ON next.

This sequence ensures safe and correct operation.

5. Sleep & Wake-Up Modes

If the petrol solenoid remains OFF for 10 minutes, the device automatically enters sleep mode to conserve power.

To wake the device, turn the power switch OFF and then ON again.

8. Precautions

Do not unplug the ThermaFog Tracker from the thermal fogging machine while it is powered ON. Disconnecting during operation may cause permanent device damage.

9. Device Care & Maintenance

  • Do not expose the device to physical impact or damage.
  • Use only manufacturer-approved accessories and chargers.
  • Do not store in extremely hot or cold environments.
  • Operating temperature range: -20°C to +70°C
  • Keep the device dry at all times.
  • Do not use outdoors during thunderstorms.
